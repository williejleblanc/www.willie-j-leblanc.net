In 2016, Willie LeBlanc decided that he was ready to take the next step in his literary career. He opened the doors to Inkwell Publications. Currently he only has one author, J. Winston, but he has many potential authors seeking a publishing home. Are you that next best selling author? Do you just need someone to help you tell your story to the world? Are you looking for a family as opposed to a publishing company that is not hands on with their authors? Well inkwell Publications is the right place for you!

 

If you would like to become part of the Inkwell family, email us at email address provided under the Contact Us tab.

Also in 2016, he figured that he could gt more readership if people were able to get a small visual of what they were buying. He searched around and found people were utilizing promotional trailers. Soon he learned how to use  software and create his own.

He began to upload them to Facebook and YouTube. People love the way that he can incorporate their vision with his own and create a cinematic style Promotional trailer for a fraction of the cost. Thus, M.S. Promotions and Videos was born!

 

For more info, follow the link in the Contact Us tab.
